Beans Recipe


A deliciously cooked plate of beans

Ingredients

  • Raw Beans (4 cups)
  • Palm Oil (10 cL)
  • Salt (1 tablespoon)
  • Onions (2 small ones, or 1 large one)
  • Grounded Pepper (1 sachet)

Steps

  1. Pick the raw beans, remove all dirt
  2. Wash the beans
  3. Put beans in a pot, and add the peeled and sliced onions
  4. Add water (2L) and boil for 25 mins
  5. Once the time has elapsed, include other ingredients such as the palm oil, pepper and salt,
    and consider adding some more water for the next 25 mins
  6. Boil for another 25 minutes (second 25 mins) then check back if the beans need more water,
    once the time has elapsed ... Consider adding enough water to last another 25 mins
  7. Boil for another 25 minutes (third 25 mins) and mash the beans when the time has elapsed
  8. After mashing let it cook for another 5 to 10 minutes
  9. Serve and Enjoy
