Boiled Potatoes Recipe


A bowl serving of some boiled fresh potatoes

Ingredients

  • Raw potatoes
  • 1 table spoon of salt

Steps

  1. Boil raw potatoes for 20 minutes to soften the potatoes and its skin
  2. Take them out of the pot put them in a bowl of clean, warm water
  3. Peel the skin
  4. Slice the potatoes in half
  5. Place peeled and sliced potatoes in a pot of water and add the salt
  6. Boil for 10 minutes
  7. Voila! Your potatoes are done. Serve with condiments and stew on top.
