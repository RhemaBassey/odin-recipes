Rice Recipe


A delicious meal of rice and stew

Ingredients

  • Raw Rice (3 cups)
  • Salt (1 table spoon)

Steps

  1. Wash your rice
  2. Add fresh water (1L)to a pot, put in the rice and let it boil
  3. Boil your rice for 10 minutes
  4. Once boiling and time has elapsed, add the salt
  5. Reduce the flames to its lowest and let it simmer for 15 minutes
  6. Once the time is reached take it out of the fire, serve with condiments and stew and enjoy
